# Svar till Polar.sh Organization Details

Här är proffsiga svar anpassade för Ticko. Kopiera och klistra in!

### Describe your business
Ticko is a gamified paper trading platform designed to educate users about the Swedish stock market. Users manage virtual portfolios, compete in leagues, and track their performance on global leaderboards in a risk-free environment.

### What do you sell?
We sell digital consumables/features for our trading game. The specific product is a "Portfolio Reset" (one-time purchase) which allows users to reset their virtual trading balance and leaderboard statistics to start fresh.

### How will you integrate Polar into your business?
We use Polar checkout for one-time payments of digital goods. The integration uses the API to initiate sessions and webhooks to automatically fulfill the service (resetting the user's game state) immediately upon successful payment.
